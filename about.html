About MJ MADE

Mandisa · Founder & Baker

Meet the Baker

"Hi, I'm Mandisa — a 19-year-old student at Botswana Accountancy College and the heart behind MJ MADE. What started as a passion for baking in my home kitchen has grown into something I truly love sharing with others."

Our Story

"MJ MADE was born from a simple idea: that every baked good should feel like a warm hug. Based in Gaborone, Botswana, we specialize in home-baked cinnamon rolls, brownies, cakes, and cookies — each made from scratch with quality ingredients and a whole lot of love. Whether it's for a celebration or just because, we've got something sweet for every moment."

"Baked in small batches. Delivered with care."
